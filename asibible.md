### HydroGuard™ Coating

Nano-ceramic hydrophobic coating applied to glass surfaces. Available for automotive, structural, and marine applications.

#### Specification
- **Type:** Nano-ceramic hydrophobic polymer
- **UV Block:** >98% UV radiation blocked (prevents polymerisation and yellowing)
- **Water Repellency:** Hydrophobic topcoat repels water, dirt, contaminants
- **Wiper Reduction:** Reduces wiper drag by up to 40% (less friction on dry glass)
- **Self-Cleaning:** Rain and water run off, carrying dust and salt deposits
- **Durability:** 12–18 months between reapplications (marine) to 24+ months (protected environments)
- **Ceramic-Infused:** Resistant to scratches and environmental damage

#### Benefits
- Up to 60% reduction in full-glass replacements (extended life through protection)
- Up to 25% drop in unscheduled downtime (less frequent cleaning and maintenance)
- Improved driver visibility (hydrophobic surface reduces haze and water spots)
- Reduced wiper wear (less friction, extended blade life)
- Integrates into existing cleaning/maintenance routines (no special equipment required)

#### Applications

**Automotive (Mass-Transit & Fleets)**
- Windscreens and side glass
- Typical cost: $200–$500 per vehicle
- Reapplication interval: 18–24 months
- Revenue model: Installation fee + recoat maintenance contracts

**Structural (Buildings & Facades)**
- External curtain walls, skylights, canopies, balustrades
- Typical cost: $1,000–$20,000 (depending on facade area)
- Reapplication interval: 12–18 months
- Revenue model: One-time installation + annual recoat contracts

**Marine (Boats & Vessels)**
- Cabin windows, portholes, deckhouse glazing
- Typical cost: $500–$3,000 per vessel
- Reapplication interval: 12–18 months (aggressive salt environment)
- Revenue model: Installation fee + quarterly/semi-annual recoat contracts

#### Installation Process
1. **Surface prep:** Clean glass thoroughly, remove contaminants
2. **Application:** Apply nano-ceramic coating using controlled technique (spray or wipe-on)
3. **Cure:** Allow 24 hours cure before exposure to water/rain
4. **Quality validation:** Water bead test (hydrophobic effect verified)
5. **Documentation:** Digital record in ASIPortal with batch lot number, application date, technician ID

#### Maintenance
- Light cleaning: Soft cloth with distilled water (no harsh chemicals)
- Regular recoat: Interval depends on environment (marine = 12–18 months; automotive = 18–24 months; structural = 12–18 months)
- Longevity: Ceramic base lasts indefinitely; hydrophobic topcoat requires periodic renewal

### APEAX Film Pricing Framework

#### Price Basis
All APEAX products are priced EX-WORKS (USA), meaning:
- **EX-WORKS:** ASI purchases from APEAX factory in USA at USD price
- **FX Conversion:** Default 1.62 AUD/USD (may fluctuate; use live FX rate for current quotes)
- **GST:** 10% Australian Goods and Services Tax (ALL portal prices are GST-inclusive)
- **Freight:** Additional uplift depending on shipping method

#### Freight Uplift (Applied After FX Conversion)
- **Airfreight:** +32% (faster, suitable for urgent orders)
- **Seafreight:** +17% (slower, typically 4–6 weeks, cost-effective for bulk orders)

#### Calculation Formula
```
Landed AUD Price = (USD Price × 1.62) × (1 + Freight %) × 1.10 (GST)
```

**Example:**
- OptiShield windscreen film: $500 USD per roll
- FX conversion: $500 × 1.62 = $810 AUD
- Airfreight +32%: $810 × 1.32 = $1,069.20 AUD
- GST: $1,069.20 × 1.10 = $1,176.12 AUD (customer price)

#### GST Portal Rendering
- **Portal prices:** Always GST-inclusive (customer sees final price)
- **Xero line-item unit price:** Divide portal price by 1.10 to get ex-GST unit amount
  - Example: $1,176.12 portal price ÷ 1.10 = $1,069.20 Xero unitAmount

**Problem:** Traditional approach treats glass as consumable — replacement every 2–3 years, then landfill disposal.

**ASI Solution:** Multi-stage remediation + protective coatings = 3–5x life extension.

- Average coach windscreen: ~$600 replacement cost

- Standard approach: 20 coaches × 5 replacements per 10-year period = 100 windscreens to landfill
