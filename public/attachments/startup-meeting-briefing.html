1:1 HumAI Teams — The Vision

Josh's vision: Every human technician paired 1:1 with a robotic system. Not replacing people — amplifying them. The first company of our kind to do this. Not just in Melbourne. Not just in Australia. Not just APAC. The world.

The immediate application: robotic scratch removal. This is ASI's most labour-intensive service — manual polishing of wiper arcs, mechanical impact scratches, and graffiti damage across curved coach glass. Perfect candidate for cobot (collaborative robot) augmentation.

Why This Makes Sense

  • Throughput: 4 buses/day (robotic) vs 1.5 buses/day (manual) — 2.7× increase
  • Margin: 75% robotic vs 34% manual — doubles the profit per bus
  • Consistency: Force-controlled polishing eliminates human fatigue variance
  • Data capture: Every movement logged — feeds ML models for continuous improvement
  • Safety: Removes repetitive strain from human techs

Unit 1 Breakdown: Dobot CR5 System

Why Dobot CR5 Over UR10e

The UR10e (Universal Robots) is the industry standard at ~$75K for the arm alone. The Dobot CR5 delivers equivalent payload (5kg), reach (900mm), and ±0.02mm repeatability at $25-40K for the arm — less than half the price. Both are ISO 10218-1 and ISO/TS 15066 compliant collaborative robots. ASI doesn't need UR's ecosystem premium — we're building our own tooling and vision system.
